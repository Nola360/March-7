Mastery

/ˈmast(ə)rē/

  1. Mastery is a high level of a skill.
  2. Possession or display of great skill or technique
  3. Skill or knowledge that makes one master of a subject : COMMAND

Only one who devotes himself to a cause with his whole strength and soul can be a true master.
For this reason mastery demands all of a person.

- Albert Einstein
